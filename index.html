Spotify Playlist Curator

This project serves as a new python full appliction project for me. The purpose of the app is to shorten the time it takes to add songs and create a well rounded playlist. Currently, the way I personally fill out playlists is I look through a playlist, find a song I like, look through recommendations and radios, then add songs from that to the playlist, hopefully the songs being added relate to the genre behind the playlist. This application serves to be a more streamlined way of doing this. Currently, the plan is to create an app that can go through this process effectively. In the future, I would like to add newer features and abilites that may be difficult to complete through the spotify desktop application.
